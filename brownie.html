The Brownie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 3 Eggs
  • 280g Sugar
  • 60g Butter
  • 160g Chocolate
  • 170g Powder

Following Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Melt the Chocolate and the Butter together, then mix it.
  3. Mix the eggs with sugar.
  4. Then combine the chocolate mixing with the eggs and sugar.
  5. After mixing it well, add the powder.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 35 minutes at 180°C.
  7. Then let it cool for a bit. Enjoy!
